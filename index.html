Digital Twin Systems
& Precision Medicine

  • Developing personalized digital twin systems that integrate multi-modal clinical data (imaging, clinical demographics, biomechanical signals, and 3D anatomical representations) for precise musculoskeletal health management.

  • Applying advanced causal inference, predictive analytics, and generative AI to perform longitudinal analyses and individualized scenario simulations.

  • Creating interactive decision-support tools that allow clinicians and researchers to explore preventive care strategies, disease trajectories, and personalized treatment plans.

  • Establishing analytical methods and software platforms to enable real-time clinical translation and improve patient-specific clinical outcomes.

Foundation Models &
Model Generalization

  • Investigating foundational AI models, including the Segment Anything Model (SAM), for generalizability across different anatomical structures, patient populations, and imaging modalities in musculoskeletal MRI.

  • Developing robust strategies for fine-tuning and adapting pre-trained models to enhance transferability and clinical utility across medical domains.

  • Conducting comprehensive evaluations to identify limitations and strengths of foundation models, informing clinical adoption and research best practices.

  • Disseminating findings through leading conferences and open-access publications to facilitate adoption by diverse research groups and clinical users.

  • GitHub Preprint

Representation Learning
& Efficient Data Utilization

  • Exploring innovative representation learning methods, including self-supervised learning (SSL), to enhance feature extraction and downstream task performance from minimally labeled datasets.

  • Analyzing the impact of embedding and representation quality on model performance, interpretability, and efficiency in limited-data contexts.

  • Developing new methods to leverage clinical text, tabular data, and imaging biomarkers, improving model accuracy and reducing dependency on costly annotated datasets.

  • Investigating fairness and bias in learned data representations to ensure equitable clinical performance across diverse demographic groups.

  • Slide Deck Video Preprint

Medical Data Systems: Strategic Insights & Clinical Operations

View More

Medical Data Systems:
Strategic Insights & Clinical Operations

  • Developing software and hardware solutions aimed at strategically collecting and utilizing medical data to monitor clinical procedures, assess quality of care, and evaluate operational efficiency.

  • Utilizing clinical data (such as video laryngoscopy, procedural outcomes, and clinician performance metrics) to reveal patterns, challenges, and opportunities for process improvement within healthcare systems.

  • Leveraging medical data analytics as a powerful tool for administrative insights, clinical education and training enhancement, and proactive hospital management, ultimately driving operational excellence.

  • Engaging directly with interdisciplinary stakeholders—clinicians, administrators, IT departments, and insurers—to ensure alignment of AI innovations with realistic hospital workflows, economic considerations, and strategic priorities.
